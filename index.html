Sara J. Tomczuk

Sara Tomczuk is a doctoral candidate in the Sociology Department at the University of Washington. She researches the sociology of culture, race & ethnicity, qualitative methods, and interethnic relations. The title of her dissertation-in-progress is "Environment of Tension, Moments of Conflict: A Comparative Study of Czech, Slovak, and Roma Interethnic Relations." As an instructor, she has taught introductory, upper-level, online, and service-learning courses. She is currently the teaching assistant at the UW Department of Sociology Writing Center.
